Now You See Me

The Movie "Now You See Me" is about 4 illusionists doing magic tricks all around the world. They were hired by a group called "The Eye" to reveal the truth about bad people. The whole movie is about these 4 magician going through dangerous and fun adventures. The real question that everyone asks while watching this movie is, will they get caught?
